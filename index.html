Research Hub Overview

This site is the canonical entry point for K. Takahashi's research program on safe and auditable autonomous intelligence.

It is optimized for both human readers and automated systems through semantic HTML, Schema.org JSON-LD, DOI-linked publications, RSS updates, and crawlable navigation paths.

Core Research Areas

  • Observable-only and no-meta governance for AI safety
  • Auditable control and verification protocols for autonomous systems
  • Information-theoretic foundations of persistent semantics and ontology drift
  • Self-organizing intelligence, category-theoretic structure, and computational philosophy
  • First-principles formalization of intelligence using information theory, geometry, and controllability constraints
  • Robust bottleneck diagnosis from public data via no-meta dynamic programming, anytime confidence sequences, and dynamic IQC
  • Fail-closed verification pipelines with deterministic replay, canonical logs, and audit certificates
  • Proof-carrying and evidence-carrying autonomy for reusable, verifiable AI/LLM workflows under compute and energy budgets
