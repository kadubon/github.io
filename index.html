Biography

As an independent researcher, I explore the mathematical and philosophical foundations of self-improving AI. My work integrates category theory, information geometry, and active inference to propose formal architectures for autonomous, adaptive, and structurally self-modifying systems.

I focus on developing non-static, recursive frameworks that enable collective AI agents to evolve their internal structure and reasoning pathways. This research, grounded in the free energy principle, aims to create AI that adapts not only by learning from data, but by reconfiguring its own representational logic and causal topology.
